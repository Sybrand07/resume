AutoHotkey Assign a hotkey to perform repetitive tasks.
  • Press e.g., "Ctrl + J" to instantly copy-paste highlighted text to a new cell in Excel.
  • Press e.g., "Ctrl + L" to give highlighted text as input to ChatGPT and return output to the clipboard once ready.
